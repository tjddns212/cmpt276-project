FAQs

General Guidelines

  • How do I create an account on the platform?
    Click on the 'Sign Up' button on the top-right corner of the homepage. Follow the prompts to enter your information. Once completed, you'll be able to create a profile and start using our platform.
  • How do I list a room for rent?
    First, you need to log into your account. Click on 'Post My Room' at the middle of the page. Fill out the form with all the necessary details about the room, including location, room size, amenities, rental price, and house rules. Once you've filled everything out, click 'Submit'. Your listing will be posted immediately on our platform
  • How can I use the roommate matching feature?
    To use the roommate matching feature, you need to complete your profile first. This includes filling out details about your preferences, interests, and lifestyle. Once your profile is complete, navigate to the 'Roommate Matching' page where you'll find suggestions of potential housemates.

General Questions

  • I'm new to Vancouver and unsure about my rights as a tenant. Where can I find this information?
    We provide a section for Housing Resources on our platform, where you can find useful guides on renting, legal rights, budgeting, and other relevant information to help you navigate the housing market in Vancouver.
  • Are there any fees associated with using the platform?
    Our platform is free to use for all registered users. There are no sign-up fees or charges for listing a room or contacting homeowners. However, any financial arrangements regarding the rental agreement are handled directly between the homeowner and the tenant.
  • I've forgotten my password. How can I reset it?
    If you've forgotten your password, click on the 'Forgot Password' link on the sign-in page. Enter the email address associated with your account and we'll send you a link to reset your password. Please ensure you have access to this email account.
  • I'm having technical issues with the website. How can I get help? Answer
    We're sorry to hear you're having issues. For technical support, please send an email to our support team. You can find the email blow the homepage. We'll do our best to resolve your issue as quickly as possible.
  • What is the Community Forum and how can I use it?
    The Community Forum is a place where users can ask questions, seek advice, and share experiences related to finding housing and living in shared accommodations. After logging in to your account, you can access the forum through the main menu. You can post new topics or respond to existing discussions.
